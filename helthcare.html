Who are the Top 25 Voices?

The existing ecosystem of healthcare robotics is witnessing pioneering scientific innovations from all sectors, including academia, corporates,startups, providers, and policymakers. These innovations have led to the economic growth of the entire healthcare robotics sector. While developments are brought about by organizations, it is the individuals who have been the harbingers of innovation by dedicating their professional lives to extraordinary pursuits of laboratory research and product development. Top 25 Voices Awards Initiative recognizes these leaders whose ‘voices’ have been able to drive innovation and improve the practices in the healthcare robotics industry.

Screening Methodology

To select the Top 25 Voices, this study had used an aggregate impact matrix-based research design, which was most suited to the context of the study (which includes the self and peer nominated leaders within the healthcare robotics ecosystem.) The selection of Top 25 Voices using an aggregate impact matrix was based on publicly available data and a balanced scorecard of leaders developed from the nominations filed by themselves and their industry peers. The research methodology includes eight core parameters to build and validate a scorecard matrix
